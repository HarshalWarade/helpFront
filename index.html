Group of Cards




A group of cards that could be used to display many cards in a div. They will align themselves to the center of the div automatically. In the property grouped_h_cards_rpt3, the 3 represent the cards to be shown in a row, or you could assume it as the number of columns the card would be displayed in. It could be changed to maximum limit of 6. And don't worry about their weird width. They'll adapt a very impressive width once the content is added to the body of the cards.

Featured Cards




This card could be used to highlight the heading of the page, or to show the priority of a website. In this example h_hgt-3 class is used, in which 3 means, set height to 30vh, you can adjust it accordingly to 10, which means the maximum height of 100vh.

Body of the featured card.

          Heading of the card
      </div>
      <div class="h_fe-body h_px-2-3">

Image Cards




You can add multiple cards to the parent div and also can arrange them the way you want. Just simply you need to add grouped_h_cards_rpt3 in your parent div holding the image cards. You can adjust the number of columns by chainging the 3 in grouped_h_cards_rpt3 upto maximum of 6.
