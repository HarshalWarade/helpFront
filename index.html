Group of Cards




A group of cards that could be used to display many cards in a div. They will align themselves to the center of the div automatically. In the property grouped_h_cards_rpt3, the 3 represent the cards to be shown in a row, or you could assume it as the number of columns the card would be displayed in. It could be changed to maximum limit of 6.

Featured Cards




This card could be used to highlight the heading of the page, or to show the priority of a website. In this example h_hgt-3 class is used, in which 3 means, set height to 30vh, you can adjust it accordingly to 10, which means the maximum height of 100vh.

Heading of the card

Body of the featured card.

<div id="h_featured" class="h_hgt-3">
  <div class="h_fe-card">
      <div class="h_fe-heading">
          Heading of the card
      </div>
      <div class="h_fe-body h_px-2-3">
          <p>Body of the featured card.</p>
      </div>
  </div>
</div>
